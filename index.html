Process

Three steps. Any space. Anywhere on Earth.

1

Scan with Your Phone

Walk through any space recording a video with Polycam, KIRI Engine, or any Gaussian Splatting capture app. Takes under 5 minutes. Works with any modern smartphone.

2

Export the .splat File

The capture app reconstructs reality into a photorealistic 3D scene. Export it as a .splat or .ply file. AI fills in every surface, shadow, and reflection from your footage.

3

Share the Link

Drop the file into our viewer and share the link. Anyone with it can walk through the space in their browser — no app, no download, no agent appointment required.
